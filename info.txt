The name of the website is Property Insight



Our project is a smart web-based real estate application providing investors with 
comprehensive, current information on property deals and trends. It offers user
friendly search capabilities based on property numbers and map locations, and 
supplies growth and trend data for informed decision-making. Utilizing AI, we 
predict future property prices based on historic data and market trends. Our system 
features a LLMs-powered Chatbot, enabling users to interact in natural language. 
This Graduation Project Report will present an overview of our "Real Estate Investor 
Advisor" website, the development challenges, solutions, and future, demonstrating 
its potential to transform real estate investing.
